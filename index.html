Our Final Project Topic

National Mandarin Competition Website

NMC (National Mandarin Competition) is one of the events held by BNMC (BINUS Mandarin Club) with the goal of improving one’s Chinese skills, and to preserve Chinese culture. NMC’s theme “Bridging Generations With Chinese Culture”, hopes that NMC can be a bridge that connect people from different generations to support the preservation and introduction of Chinese culture towards society.

We plan to make a fully functional app to support this competition, from the registration process until management. We will also include a functional helpdesk ticketing system to help with the problems that the participants may encounter.
